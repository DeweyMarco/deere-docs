## Share Intel with Smart Machinery

Does your software create variable rate prescription files for equipment execution?

Make the most out of equipment connectivity to wirelessly deliver prescription files straight to the cab. Your prescription files ensure the right seed planted at the right time, with the right precision.

Retrieve the data you need to create the file then share the prescription with the farmer for best-in-class field execution. These files help farmers to harness the power of Section Control, Exact Apply Nozzle Control, See & Spray Ultimate, and See & Spray Select for Seeding, Planting and Spraying.

---

## How to Send Files to Operations Center and Machines

<Steps>
  <Step title="Retrieve customer data from Operations Center">
    Pull the data needed to build your prescription file:

    - **Boundaries** — field boundary data for the target area
    - **Previous Year's Harvest** — historical yield data to inform variable rate decisions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the prescription file in your software">
    Use the retrieved data to generate your variable rate prescription file.

    Refer to the [Files Guide](#) for supported formats and specifications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the file to the farmer">
    Deliver the prescription file to the farmer's Operations Center account and/or directly to the farmer's machine for execution.
  </Step>
</Steps>
