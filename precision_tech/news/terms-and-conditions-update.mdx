title: "Terms & Conditions Update (Feb 12, 2020)"
---

### What is changing?

Organizations can now designate which staff members have the authority to manage organization access (Level 2), including entering into contracts with John Deere and accepting terms and conditions.

Each organization will be presented with a new Terms & Conditions acceptance flow upon login to Operations Center. Acceptance will require the organization user to provide or review the following:

- Legal Entity Name and Address
- Review Organization Staff (Users)
- Review Partnerships (Permissions)
- Review Equipment with JDLink Service contracts
- Review terminals to ensure they are in the correct location
- Review subscriptions
- Accept or decline Organization Terms and Conditions and Privacy Notices

### Impact of Non-Acceptance

Customers will not be able to do the following until these Terms are accepted:

- Access John Deere Operations Center, JDLink Dashboard, mobile apps, etc.
- Transfer new terminals into the organization
- Make changes to subscriptions — activate, renew, or change
- Allow Connected Software Companies to access their data via APIs (beginning August 13, 2020)

### Required Customer Action (RCA)

This Terms change is one example of what we are calling a **Required Customer Action (RCA)** event. As a Connected Software Company servicing customers, you will need to prepare your applications to handle possible errors passed through our APIs when a required customer action event has not been handled properly or in a timely manner.

Please see the RCA documentation for instructions on how to handle these events in your application.

If you need more information about Terms & Conditions, or need help preparing your application to handle Required Customer Action events, please use the **Support** button at the bottom of the page.
