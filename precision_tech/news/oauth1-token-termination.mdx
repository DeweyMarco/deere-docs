title: "OAuth 2 Token Termination (Jan 1, 2021)"
---

### What is changing?

Make your changes before **May 1, 2021** to keep access to customer data and avoid OAuth 2 token termination.

- OAuth 2 technical documentation can be found at [developer.deere.com](https://developer.deere.com).
- Stay informed about John Deere APIs by keeping team members' contact information associated with your application current in the **My Applications** section of [developer.deere.com](https://developer.deere.com).
- If you need assistance, contact our API Support Team by clicking the **Support** button at the bottom of the page.
