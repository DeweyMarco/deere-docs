## Authentication

All John Deere APIs use **OAuth 2.0** with bearer tokens. Include your token in every request:

```bash
Authorization: Bearer <access_token>
```

<Note>
  Contact your John Deere partner representative to obtain API credentials and access to the sandbox environment.
</Note>
